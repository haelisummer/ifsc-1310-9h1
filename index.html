    Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    Reading about progressive enhancement helped me realize how important it is to focus on the content of you page more. Although the design stands out, progressive enhancement ensures that all users get a comfortable feel from the content given. The Peanut M & M example gave me a deeper understanding. Especially considering I'm one of those who like that kind of M & M particularly because of the peanut in the inside itself, which was used to represent the content of a site. Helping me understand if it were me the content of the site would matter over the design of it. The design would draw me in but it would be what is stated in the content that keeps me drawn in.
